Restaurants

Ithaa Undersea Restaurant:

Ithaa Undersea Restaurant at Conrad Maldives Rangali Island offers a unique dining experience beneath the waves. Situated 16 feet below sea level, this iconic restaurant boasts panoramic views of coral gardens and marine life. Serving contemporary European cuisine with an emphasis on seafood, Ithaa delights diners with a multi-course tasting menu featuring locally and globally sourced ingredients. With its intimate setting and unparalleled underwater views, Ithaa Undersea Restaurant is a must-visit destination for travelers seeking culinary excellence in the Maldives.


Nihonbashi Blue:

Nihonbashi Blue at Nihonbashi Restaurant, Vella Private Island, offers a sophisticated Japanese dining experience in an overwater pavilion. Led by Chef Haru Inukai, the restaurant specializes in sushi and teppanyaki, showcasing fresh seafood and premium cuts of meat. With its elegant ambiance and panoramic lagoon views, Nihonbashi Blue provides an unforgettable culinary journey through Japanese cuisine.






HOTELS

Cheval Blanc Randheli:

Cheval Blanc Randheli is a luxurious resort located in the pristine Noonu Atoll of the Maldives. Renowned for its unparalleled elegance and impeccable service, the resort offers discerning guests an exquisite tropical retreat. The resort's exclusive villas, each with its own private pool and dedicated Majordomo, epitomize luxury living in paradise. Culinary delights await at the resort's restaurants, including the Michelin-starred Le 1947, where guests can savor gourmet French cuisine paired with exceptional wines. With its stunning overwater villas, pristine beaches, and world-class amenities, Cheval Blanc Randheli promises an unforgettable escape in the heart of the Indian Ocean.


Rixos Premium Hotels:

Rixos Premium Hotels are renowned for their exceptional luxury and hospitality experiences. With stunning locations and elegant accommodations, they provide a haven for discerning travelers seeking relaxation and indulgence. From spacious rooms and suites with breathtaking views to exquisite dining options and world-class amenities, Rixos Premium Hotels prioritize guest satisfaction. Whether it's unwinding at the lavish spa, enjoying recreational activities, or experiencing vibrant nightlife, these hotels offer a wealth of opportunities for guests of all preferences. With their commitment to excellence and elevated hospitality, Rixos Premium Hotels have established themselves as a symbol of luxury, creating cherished memories for guests and delivering an unforgettable stay.






Monuments

Sultan Park:

Sultan Park in Malé, the capital of the Maldives, offers a serene retreat amidst the bustling cityscape. Once the site of the Sultan's Palace, this historic park features shaded pathways, lush gardens, and remnants of the palace's architecture. It provides a tranquil escape for both locals and tourists to relax and enjoy the peaceful ambiance in the heart of Malé.


Maldives National Museum:

The Maldives National Museum, located in the capital city of Malé, offers visitors a fascinating glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of the Maldives. Housed in the former Sultan's Palace, the museum showcases a diverse collection of artifacts spanning the country's history, including royal regalia, ancient manuscripts, and traditional Maldivian costumes. With its informative exhibits and beautifully preserved artifacts, the Maldives National Museum provides a captivating journey through the archipelago's past, offering visitors a deeper understanding of its unique traditions and customs.
